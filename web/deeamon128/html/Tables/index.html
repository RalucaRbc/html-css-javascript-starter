My review on some of the countries I have visited

Name Year Opinion Worth visiting? Yes/No
Laos 2017 One of the most beautiful country I have visited.
Worth going for jungles, falls, coffee plantations and people		 Yes
Vietnam 2017 Awesome history, temples, ruins. The food was cheap and people way too nice. Yes
Cambodia 2017 Beautiful islands, Angor Wat and the rest of the temples, learnt about Pol Pot and Red Khmer history Yes
Morroco 2016 Worth going for history, Sahara desert trip, Chefchauen and Fes - Unesco heritage, and for riads and hamman
Super unsafe, strict dress code, dodgy people everywhere, got scammed many times		 No, unless you go by tour or with a big group of family/friends
Egypt 2019 Cairo, Abu Simbel, Luxor, Aswan. Rich in history, culture, interesting nightlife No, unless going by tour. Felt unsafe in many places
Kenya 2018 Diany beach, safari, Nairobi, Mombasa, Expensive country, but worth the money.
the cities are too polluted and I recommend going to the coast side or safari		 Yes. Unsafe overall, but always be cautious
